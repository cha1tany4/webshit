**Last updated:** March 2026

## What We Collect

We use [GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com/) for privacy-respecting analytics. GoatCounter collects:

- Page URL visited
- Referrer (where you came from)
- Browser and OS type (aggregated)
- Country (from IP, not stored)
- Screen size

GoatCounter does **not** use cookies, does not track individuals across sessions, and does not share data with third parties.

We collect nothing else. No email addresses. No accounts. No tracking pixels. No fingerprinting.

## Browser Storage

We store one item in your browser's local storage: your **theme preference** (light or dark mode). This is set only if you use the theme toggle. It contains no personal data, never leaves your device, and is used solely to remember your display choice between visits.

## What We Don't Do

- No advertising
- No retargeting
- No third-party scripts (except GoatCounter's single JS file)
- No newsletter or email collection
- No social media tracking buttons

## Your Rights

You can block GoatCounter's script with any ad blocker. The site works perfectly without it — we won't miss the data.

## Contact

Open a GitHub issue if you have privacy concerns.
